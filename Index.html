About Dave Stitches

For over 3 years, Dave Stitches has been the trusted name for quality tailoring in Ikorodu and Lagos. We combine traditional craftsmanship with modern style to create clothes that fit perfectly and make you feel confident.

Our commitment to local craftsmanship means every piece is made with care, attention to detail, and understanding of what Nigerian women want in their wardrobe.
